About Edged Canvas

Crafting functional art with Canadian pride

Our Story

Founded in Vancouver with a passion for merging aesthetics with functionality, Edged Canvas brings together traditional craftsmanship and innovative design. We specialize in creating pieces that serve practical purposes while enhancing your space visually.

Our Philosophy

We believe everyday objects deserve thoughtful design. Our focus on acoustic sound panels has allowed us to develop unique solutions that transform problematic spaces while adding artistic elements that inspire.

Local Craftsmanship

As a small Canadian business, we take pride in sourcing materials locally and supporting our community's economy. Every piece is handcrafted in our Vancouver workshop, ensuring quality and attention to detail.
