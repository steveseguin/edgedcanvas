About Edged Canvas

Founded in Canada with a passion for functional art, Edged Canvas brings together craftsmanship and practical design. We specialize in creating pieces that serve a purpose while enhancing your space aesthetically.

Our focus on acoustic sound panels for businesses and homes has allowed us to develop unique solutions that combine visual appeal with acoustic performance.

As a small local business, we take pride in sourcing materials within Canada and supporting our local economy.
